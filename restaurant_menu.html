Katsu Sando

This culinary delight features a tender, juicy pork cutlet, expertly breaded and fried to golden perfection. Nestled between two slices of soft, pillowy Japanese milk bread, the cutlet is complemented by a layer of crisp, fresh cabbage and drizzled with a savory, tangy tonkatsu sauce.

Price: $8.95

Fried Dumplings

Perfectly crisp on the outside and delectably tender on the inside. Each dumpling is lovingly hand-folded and filled with a savory mixture of seasoned pork, fresh vegetables, and aromatic spices. Served with a side of tangy soy dipping sauce, these golden delights offer a satisfying crunch with every bite, making them the perfect appetizer or snack to complement any meal.

Price: $12.50

Sashimi

This delicacy showcases the finest cuts of fresh, high-quality fish, artfully sliced to perfection. Each piece is a testament to our commitment to authenticity and tradition, offering a melt-in-your-mouth texture that highlights the natural sweetness and delicate flavors of the seafood. Accompanied by a touch of wasabi and a splash of premium soy sauce, our Sashimi provides a clean, elegant taste experience that will transport you to the heart of Japanese culinary excellence.

Price: $18.00

Ebi Curry

The plump, juicy shrimp are cooked to perfection and immersed in a fragrant curry made from a blend of spices, vegetables, and a hint of sweetness. Served over a bed of fluffy steamed rice, this dish offers a harmonious blend of savory, sweet, and spicy notes that will tantalize your taste buds.

Price: $14.00
